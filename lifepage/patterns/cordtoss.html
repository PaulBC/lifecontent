Caber tosser
Population in generation t is asymptotic to  5 log(t)/log(2).

A glider bounces back and forth between a Cordership and a fixed
reflector; the round trip time doubles with each trip.  Whenever
the glider hits the fixed reflector, an extra glider is created;
these gliders eventually dominate the population.
Dean Hickerson, dean@ucdmath.ucdavis.edu  5/1/91
