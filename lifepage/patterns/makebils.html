Glider syntheses for 4 billiard tables
A collection of 4 glider syntheses, found by David Buckingham,
of billiard table oscillators:
12 gliders -> p8 Hertz oscillator
14 gliders -> p3 MIT oscillator
10 gliders -> p2 scrubber
6 gliders -> p3 (unnamed?)
(Collection assembled by Dean Hickerson, 1/25/93.  The syntheses
are much older.)
