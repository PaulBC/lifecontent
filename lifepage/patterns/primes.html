Primer, a prime number sieve
Produces a westward stream of lightweight spaceships representing
prime numbers:  a LWSS escapes past the pentadecathlon around
generation  120n+100  if and only if n is prime.
This is probably not a good way to look for the largest primes!
Dean Hickerson, dean@ucdmath.ucdavis.edu  11/1/91
