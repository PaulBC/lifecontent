The 158 period 3 pseudo-oscillators up to 25 bits without any known syntheses

Unfortunately, due to time limitations, these cannot yet be shown. Most consist of a small still-life on as as-yet-unsynthesized period-3 oscillator. There are also a few consisting of two oscillators (caterer and/or jam) with adjacent rotors.

There are 2 22-bit ones:

  • 2 Trans skewed poles on block.

There are 8 23-bit ones:

  • 4 Trans skewed poles on boat.
  • 4 Fire spitting or bowed caterer on block.

There are 45 24-bit ones:

  • 18 Trans skewed poles on beehive, ship, snake or carrier.
  • 8 Fire spitting or bowed caterer on boat.
  • 16 Block on 20-bit P3 oscillator.
  • 3 Caterer on caterer (there are four; one can be synthesized).
