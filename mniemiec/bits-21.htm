The 22134 21-bit period 2 pseudo-oscillators

Due to space limitations, these are shown without syntheses on a separate page.

All of these can be synthesized using the same techniques that are used for smaller pseudo-oscillators and pseudo-still-lifes, except one non-trivial one (shown below) and 169 trivial ones (not shown below) that involve adding a small still-life or oscillator to a large complex period 2 oscillator that does not yet have a synthesis:

  • 36 with 6-bit still-life one of three 15-bit period 2 oscillators.
  • 16 with beacon on one of three 15-bit period 2 oscillators.
  • 33 with boat on of ten 16-bit period 2 oscillators.
  • 84 with block on one of 21 17-bit period 2 oscillators.

Here are syntheses (or lack thereof) for 11 of these. All of the expensive ones were once on the "non-trivial without synthesis" list for a long time, but were eventually solved. Note that these, plus the ones without syntheses, all share three mechanisms that are all very difficult to synthesize: a beacon sandwiched between two other pieces, a bipole with pieces wrapped around it, or a cis python whose free end is near something else.
