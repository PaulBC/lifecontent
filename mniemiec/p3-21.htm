The 420 21-bit period 3 oscillators

All 420 21-bit period 3 oscillators can be synthesized except two. Unfortunately, due to time limitations, only 218 syntheses have been completed so far. The following syntheses have been completed and are shown below:

  • 79 Candelfrobra on 9-bit still-life or pseudo-still-life.
  • 12 Candelfrobra on long bookend w/tub or hook.
  • 8 Candelfrobra siamese snake or carrier on boat.
  • 8 Candelfrobra siamese python, eater or bookend on block or tub.
  • 8 Candelfrobra w/tail on boat.
  • 11 Unusual candelfrobra configurations.
  • 39 Cuphook variants.
  • 23 Jam based on 15-bit still-life.
  • 12 Jam on cap or long bookend.
  • 3 Mango jams.
  • 1 Larger pulsar quadrant.
  • 1 Larger Beluchenko's P3 with super mango.
  • 2 Larger French kiss.
  • 4 Skewed pole on key w/tail.
  • 4 Larger trice tongs.
  • 1 Larger fire spitting (no synthesis).
  • 1 Caterer variant (no synthesis).

The following additional syntheses have not yet been completed; all can be synthesized using the same techniques that are used for smaller still-lifes and period-3 oscillators:

  • 65 Cuphook w/tail variants.
  • 105 Eater eating 14-bit still-life.
  • 17 Integral eating 12-bit still-life.
  • 16 Integral w/tub or hook eating 11-bit still-life (1 shown).
