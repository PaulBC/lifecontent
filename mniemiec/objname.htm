Due to the large number of objects, this list includes only the following:

  • All objects up to 11 bits.
  • All objects known to be buildable from five gliders or less.
  • All non-factorable named objects up to 15 bits.
  • All non-factorable objects above 15 bits with constructions shown here.
  • Some larger objects with long-standing traditional names.

It typically does not include the extreme proliferation of larger objects:

  • Unnamed objects referenced only by number, size, and/or period (e.g. 12.121, 12-bit flip-flop #1) These can be found more easily on the Still-life page, or other similar pages.
  • Objects that can be factored into several separately-named similar parts (e.g. block on mango, tripole tie ship, up bipole on table).
  • Trivial expansions of smaller objects (e.g. fumarole w/tub, eater eating integral).
  • Objects referred to only by their discoverer's name, unless such unnamed objects otherwise feature prominently (e.g. Elkies's P5).
  • Objects from CA rules other than Life. (All such rules on this site occupy a single page, making it much easier to find objects by name on them).
